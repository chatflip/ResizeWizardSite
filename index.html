About Resize Wizard

Resize Wizard is a powerful Chrome extension that allows you to quickly and easily resize images right in your browser. Whether you're preparing images for social media, adjusting photos for your website, or simply need to change image dimensions, Resize Wizard has got you covered.

Key Features

  • Secure local processing - your images never leave your device
  • Intuitive drag-and-drop interface
  • Resize by exact dimensions or ratio
  • Preview thumbnail before resizing
  • Supports various image formats
  • Fast and efficient - resize images in seconds

How to Use

  1. Click on the Resize Wizard icon in your Chrome toolbar
  2. Drag and drop an image or click to select a file
  3. Choose to resize by specific dimensions or ratio
  4. Enter your desired size or ratio
  5. Click "Resize Image" and save your resized image
