About Resize Wizard

Resize Wizard is a powerful Chrome extension that allows you to quickly and easily resize images right in your browser. Whether you're preparing images for social media, adjusting photos for your website, or simply need to change image dimensions, Resize Wizard has got you covered.

Key Features

Secure Local Processing

Your images never leave your device

Intuitive Interface

Easy-to-use drag-and-drop functionality

Flexible Resizing

Resize by exact dimensions or ratio

Live Preview

See thumbnail before finalizing

How to Use

1

Click on the Resize Wizard icon in your Chrome toolbar

2

Drag and drop an image or click to select a file

3

Choose to resize by specific dimensions or ratio

4

Enter your desired size or ratio

5

Click "Resize Image" and save your resized image

Privacy First

At Resize Wizard, we take your privacy seriously. All image processing is done locally on your device, ensuring that your images are never uploaded to any server. Resize with confidence, knowing your data remains private and secure.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024/08/12

1. Information Collection and Use

Resize Wizard Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or image data. All image processing is performed locally on your device.

2. Data Storage

No user data or images are stored by the extension. Once an image is resized, the original and resized versions remain solely on your device.

3. Third-party Access

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect or store any user data.
