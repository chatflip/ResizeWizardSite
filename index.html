Resize Images with Ease

A powerful Chrome extension for quick and secure image resizing

Key Features

  • Secure Local Processing

    Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

  • Intuitive Interface

    Easy-to-use drag-and-drop functionality for effortless resizing.

  • Flexible Resizing

    Resize by exact dimensions or ratio to suit your needs.

  • Live Preview

    See the result before finalizing to ensure perfection.

How to Use

  1. Click the Resize Wizard icon in Chrome
  2. Drop or select an image
  3. Choose resize method
  4. Set dimensions or ratio
  5. Click "Resize" and save

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024/08/12

1. Information Collection and Use

Resize Wizard Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or image data. All image processing is performed locally on your device.

2. Data Storage

No user data or images are stored by the extension. Once an image is resized, the original and resized versions remain solely on your device.

3. Third-party Access

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect or store any user data.
